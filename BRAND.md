# BRAND.md — Afterset Visual Identity for Remotion Videos

## Colors
- Background: #0a0e1a (midnight)
- Card/surface: #111827 (midnight-light)
- Primary accent: #E8C547 (honey-gold) — used for emphasis words, counters, CTAs
- Secondary accent: #3b82f6 (electric blue) — used for contrast, secondary stats
- Danger/pain: #ef4444 (red) — used for negative stats, "before" states
- Success: #22c55e (green) — used sparingly for positive outcomes
- Text primary: #ffffff
- Text secondary: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.7)
- Text muted: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.5)

## Fonts (load via Google Fonts CDN or local)
- Display/headings: Bricolage Grotesque (800 ExtraBold for emphasis, 400 for normal)
- Body: DM Sans (400, 500)
- Mono/labels: Space Mono (400, uppercase for eyebrow labels)

## Visual Effects
- Ambient glow: radial-gradient gold (top-left) + blue (bottom-right), very subtle (opacity 0.05–0.08)
- CRT scan-line texture: repeating horizontal lines (optional, adds venue feel)
- Glass card surfaces: rgba(255,255,255,0.05) background + rgba(255,255,255,0.1) border
- Text shadows: 0 4px 30px rgba(0,0,0,0.5) on large text for depth

## TikTok Production Specs
- Canvas: 1080×1920 (9:16 vertical)
- FPS: 30
- Codec: H.264
- Target length: 20–35 seconds per video
- Visual change every 2–4 seconds minimum
- Text: 80–100px for hook text, 48–64px for body, 36–48px for captions
- All text must be readable with sound off
- Persistent "afterset.net" watermark (bottom-right corner, low opacity)

## Tone
- Confident, direct, slightly urgent
- NOT corporate, NOT bubbly, NOT generic SaaS
- Think: "backstage at a great venue" — moody, high-contrast, premium
